Curry Rice

Description

Japanese curry or カレー is a thick curry with a stew-like consistency and commonly includes a protein, sweet onions, carrots, and potatoes.

Ingredients

  • Japanese Curry Roux
  • 1x Apple
  • 1x Carrot
  • 1x Potato
  • 1x Onion

Steps

  1. Dice ingredients
  2. Put the vegetables to boil
  3. In a seperate large pan, stirfry onion
  4. When onion turns golden brown, pour the boiled vegetables into the pan
  5. Add roux and stir well
  6. The curry is now ready!
  7. Serve with rice and freshly plucked Panda from the zoo
  8. Itadakimasu~! Enjoy your meal!
